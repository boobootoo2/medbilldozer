## What the Landscape Shows

### 1. Existing tools are reactive
Most consumer tools engage **after** a billing failure has already caused confusion, debt, or collections. They help patients cope with errors rather than systematically detect them early.

---

### 2. Automation is shallow or absent
Many tools described as “AI-powered” still rely on:
- Human review
- Single-document analysis
- Cost benchmarking rather than reconciliation

This limits speed, scalability, and consistency.

---

### 3. No tool reconciles the full billing chain
Billing errors rarely live in a single document. They emerge **between documents**, such as:
- Bills that don’t match EOBs
- Allowed amounts that don’t match patient responsibility
- Duplicate charges across encounters
- Eligible reimbursements that are never claimed

Most existing tools look at only one artifact at a time.

## Why medBillDozer Is Different

**medBillDozer is built around a different core insight:**

> Medical billing errors are reconciliation failures, not comprehension failures.
