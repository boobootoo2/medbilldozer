## Risk Factors & Contingencies

### Fundraising Risks
- **Seed round delay**: Could push v0.5 by 2-3 months
  - *Mitigation*: Begin investor outreach during v0.3/v0.4
- **Series A delay**: Could push v0.7-v1.0 by 3-6 months
  - *Mitigation*: Demonstrate strong pilot customer traction in v0.6

### Regulatory Risks
- **Compliance complexity**: MVP phase could extend 4-8 weeks
  - *Mitigation*: Early engagement with compliance consultants during v0.4
- **Audit failures**: Could require additional iteration cycles
  - *Mitigation*: Build in 20% buffer time for v0.5-v0.6

### Market Risks
- **Slow pilot adoption**: Could delay Series A fundraising
  - *Mitigation*: Target 3-5 pilot customers during v0.5-v0.6
